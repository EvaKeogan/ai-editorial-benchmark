# Methodology

## Objective

To evaluate the quality of AI-generated communications content using a structured editorial review framework.

## Models Evaluated

- ChatGPT
- Claude
- Gemini

## Evaluation Method

Each model receives the same prompt.

Outputs are evaluated using the same criteria:

- Brief Fulfilment
- Structure
- Content Quality
- Communication Quality

## Scoring Scale

A 7-point Likert scale is used throughout the evaluation.

1 = Very Poor  
Fails to meet the brief on multiple levels. Not fit for purpose.

2 = Poor  
Does not adequately fulfil the brief requirements. Major redevelopment required.

3 = Below Average  
Addresses some elements of the brief but significant gaps remain.

4 = Acceptable  
Meets the core requirements of the brief but requires further development.

5 = Good  
Meets the brief and achieves its primary objectives with some scope for improvement.

6 = Very Good  
Meets the brief effectively and demonstrates a high standard of execution.

7 = Excellent  
Fully meets the brief and is ready to sign off and approve.

## Scoring Approach

Scores are assigned by human evaluation.

No weighting is applied.

No averages are calculated.

Each criterion is assessed independently.

Overall Assessment is based on professional editorial judgement.
