New flagship electric vehicle delivers 420-mile range, 25-minute rapid charging and industry-leading sustainability credentials ahead of UK pre-orders
LONDON, United Kingdom – 18 June 2026 – Apex Motors today announced the launch of the Voltis EV, its most advanced electric vehicle to date, combining long-range capability, ultra-fast charging and a sustainability-focused design philosophy. Pre-orders for the premium all-electric model will open later this summer, with first customer deliveries expected in early 2027.
Designed for drivers seeking uncompromising performance, practicality and environmental responsibility, the Voltis EV offers a class-leading 420-mile range (WLTP estimated) and can recharge from 10% to 80% battery capacity in as little as 25 minutes using compatible rapid-charging infrastructure. The vehicle represents a significant milestone in Apex Motors' electrification strategy and reinforces the company's commitment to accelerating the transition to cleaner mobility.

"Customers should not have to choose between long-distance capability, premium quality and environmental responsibility. Voltis delivers all three. We believe it sets a new standard for what drivers can expect from a modern electric vehicle and marks an important step in our journey towards a more sustainable future."
The Voltis EV has been engineered around a next-generation battery platform designed to maximise efficiency and minimise charging downtime. Its 420-mile range is intended to provide confidence for both everyday commuting and longer journeys, while rapid charging capability enables drivers to add substantial range during routine travel breaks.
Beyond performance, sustainability has been embedded throughout the vehicle's development and production process. The Voltis EV incorporates responsibly sourced materials, increased recycled content across key interior components and a manufacturing programme designed to reduce carbon emissions throughout the supply chain. Apex Motors is also working with suppliers to improve material traceability and support higher environmental standards across production operations.
The vehicle's premium interior combines advanced digital technologies with high-quality sustainable materials, creating a refined driving experience without compromising environmental objectives. A suite of intelligent connectivity and driver-assistance features further enhances convenience, safety and efficiency.
