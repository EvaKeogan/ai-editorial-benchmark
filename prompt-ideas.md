# Prompt Ideas

## Press Release

Write a press release announcing the launch of a new electric vehicle.

## CEO Statement

Write a CEO statement responding to a data breach.

## Product Launch

Write a product announcement for a new AI-powered productivity app.

## Crisis Communications

Write a company response to a product recall.

## Industry Commentary

Write a media comment on the impact of AI on UK businesses.

## Executive Summary

Summarise a 1,500-word business report for a board of directors in no more than 250 words.
